

## Phonology

Consonants: p, m, t, s, n, l, sh, k, g, y, w
Vowels: a, ax, i, u, ux, e, o

- a -> paw
- uh -> cut
- i -> teeth
- u -> two
- ux -> book
- e -> meh
- o -> toe
