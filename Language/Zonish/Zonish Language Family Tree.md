
**Zonish**:
- **Sotic**: SOV, phonotactic rules become stricter (limited vowel enders), more diphthongs, u moves back, ü becomes shwa, some tones added
- **Dzanian**: SVO, phonotactic rules expand (voiced consonants, more consonants: r,ts,dz,j,dj,tsh,... & clusters), ü disappears
- **Zu**: OSV/SVO, phonotactic rules heavily simplified (ends dropped), similar sounds to Zonish, vowels simplify, fewer diphthongs, hand symbols
